About Me

I am a Mathematics teacher at Mapawa National High School, handling Grade 8 students. I also serve as the Supreme Secondary Learner Government (SSLG) adviser and the school's ICT coordinator. Passionate about empowering students through both academics and leadership, I strive to make learning engaging and meaningful.

As an SSLG adviser, I guide student leaders in planning and implementing projects that promote school spirit, discipline, and community involvement. I believe in the importance of student voice and leadership in creating a positive and inclusive school environment.

As the ICT coordinator, I help integrate technology into classroom learning and support both teachers and students in using digital tools effectively. I'm committed to bridging the gap between traditional teaching and modern innovations to better prepare our learners for the future.
